About

Aida is a writer, journalist, and creator of things. With a B.A. in English from UC Berkeley, she has since engaged target audiences through her writing and editing positions. As an immigrant from Azerbaijan, she is fluent in both Russian and English. In the course of her career, she has written for various online and print medias in a variety of styles and genres. Her passions include all things lifestyle related, online culture, art, and education.
